Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We only collect data that you directly input into the application:

  • Account Information: Your name, email, and password for authentication purposes.
  • Financial Data: Expense descriptions, amounts, and dates that you log to track your spending.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to deliver the core functionality of Spendly, such as:

  • Account management and secure authentication.
  • Generating your personalized spending visualizations and reports.
  • Tracking expenditures against your monthly budget.

3. Data Storage

All recorded data is stored in the Spendly database. We take reasonable care to keep your information safe, but remember that no electronic system is perfectly secure. We recommend using a unique and strong password for your account.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or lease your financial history to advertisers or independent brokers. We only share information when legally required or when essential to operating our infrastructure (like hosting providers).

5. Contact Us

Got questions? We're happy to help. You can reach out to our team at support@spendly.track if you have any feedback or privacy concerns.
